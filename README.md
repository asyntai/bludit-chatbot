# Asyntai - AI Chatbot for Bludit

Create and launch AI assistant/chatbot for your Bludit website in minutes. It talks to your visitors, helps, explains, never misses a chat and can increase conversion rates! All while knowing your website, customized just for you. Your Bludit website can now talk.

This plugin embeds the Asyntai chatbot on your Bludit site and provides a simple admin interface to connect your site to Asyntai.


## Why choose Asyntai?

* **Increase conversions**: Instant, human like replies keep shoppers engaged and buying.

* **Never miss a chat**: The AI replies day and night, even when your team is offline.

* **Knows your website**: Customized just for you; it follows your instructions.

* **Works in all languages**: Automatically detects and answers in the visitor's language.

* **Fast responses (1-3s)**: Keeps customers from bouncing.

## Installation

1. Download the plugin and extract the `asyntai` folder
2. Upload the `asyntai` folder to your Bludit `/bl-plugins/` directory
3. Go to your Bludit admin panel
4. Navigate to Settings -> Plugins
5. Find "Asyntai - AI Chatbot" and click "Activate"

## Configuration

After installation:

1. Click on "Asyntai - AI Chatbot" in the admin sidebar
2. Click "Get started" to connect your Asyntai account
3. Sign in or create a free account at asyntai.com
4. The plugin will automatically save your connection
5. The chatbot is now live on your site!

## Managing Your Chatbot

Once connected, you can manage your chatbot settings, review chat logs, and customize AI responses at:
https://asyntai.com/dashboard

## Requirements

- Bludit 3.x or higher
- PHP 5.6 or higher

**Note:** If your Bludit version is not compatible, you can still use Asyntai by getting a JavaScript snippet from [asyntai.com/dashboard](https://asyntai.com/dashboard) and manually inserting it into your theme.
